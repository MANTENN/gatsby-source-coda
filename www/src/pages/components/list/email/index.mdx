Cards are used to callout certain features or actions Thumbtack is requesting of the recipient.

### Basic HTML lists

```email
<ul>
    <li>item one</li>
    <li>
        item two ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor
        incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.
    </li>
    <li>item three</li>
</ul>
```

```email
<ol>
    <li>item one</li>
    <li>
        item two ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor
        incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.
    </li>
    <li>item three</li>
</ol>
```
